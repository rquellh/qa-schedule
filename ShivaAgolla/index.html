New rules for smarter and faster testing
The World Quality Report is the only global report analyzing software testing and quality engineering trends. It presents an analysis of developments in agile and DevOps, artificial intelligence, automation, test environments, data, security and budgets, showing once again the importance of quality, and of the measures that are put in place to maintain it with sector focus. The 11th Edition of this report captures insight from interviews with 1,725 CIOs and senior technology leaders from 32 countries and 10 industries.
